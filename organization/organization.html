Organization Structure

Meet the leaders driving robotics innovation in the UAE

RoboCup UAE Federation

RoboCup UAE is governed by a dedicated team of robotics experts, educators, and industry leaders who are committed to advancing robotics and AI education throughout the United Arab Emirates. Our organizational structure ensures effective governance, technical excellence, and strategic alignment with national objectives.

Governance Structure

Executive Committee

Provides strategic direction and oversees overall operations of RoboCup UAE. Meets quarterly to review progress and set policies.

Technical Committee

Responsible for technical standards, rule development, and competition organization. Ensures alignment with international RoboCup standards.

Education Committee

Develops educational programs, curricula, and outreach initiatives. Coordinates with schools and universities across the UAE.

Industry Relations Committee

Manages partnerships with industry, facilitates technology transfer, and promotes commercial applications of robotics research.

RoboCup UAE

Advancing robotics and AI in the United Arab Emirates through competitive innovation and education.
