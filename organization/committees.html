Committees

Specialized teams driving excellence in robotics education and competition

RoboCup UAE operates through specialized committees that ensure effective governance, technical excellence, and strategic implementation of our mission. Each committee brings together experts from academia, industry, and government to advance robotics and AI in the UAE.

Technical Committee

Ensures technical excellence and maintains international standards across all RoboCup UAE activities

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain technical rules and regulations
  • Oversee competition infrastructure and equipment standards
  • Coordinate with international RoboCup technical committees
  • Evaluate and approve new technologies and platforms
  • Provide technical support to participating teams

Develops educational programs and promotes robotics literacy across all educational levels

  • Develop educational resources and materials
  • Establish partnerships with educational institutions

Committee Coordination

Inter-Committee Collaboration

Committees work closely together through regular joint meetings and shared initiatives. Cross-committee working groups address complex issues that span multiple areas of expertise.

Reporting Structure

All committees report to the Board of Trustees through quarterly reports and annual presentations. Committee chairs participate in board meetings to ensure alignment with strategic objectives.

Meeting Schedule

Each committee meets monthly, with additional meetings as needed. Annual committee retreats facilitate strategic planning and cross-committee collaboration.
