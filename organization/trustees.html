Board of Trustees

Distinguished leaders guiding RoboCup UAE's strategic vision

The RoboCup UAE Board of Trustees comprises distinguished leaders from academia, industry, and government who provide strategic oversight and ensure the organization's alignment with national objectives and international standards.

Photo

Dr. Ahmed Al-Mansoori

Chairman of the Board & President

UAE University

Dr. Al-Mansoori is a distinguished robotics researcher and educator with over 15 years of experience in autonomous systems and artificial intelligence. He holds a Ph.D. in Robotics from MIT and has published over 100 peer-reviewed papers in leading robotics journals.

As the former Director of the UAE National Robotics Program, he played a pivotal role in establishing the country's robotics research infrastructure. His research focuses on multi-agent systems, autonomous navigation, and human-robot interaction.

Key Achievements:

  • Established UAE's first robotics research center
  • Led 5 major international robotics projects
  • Recipient of UAE Innovation Award 2022
  • Published 100+ research papers
Photo

Prof. Fatima Al-Zahra

Vice Chairman

Khalifa University

Prof. Al-Zahra is a leading AI specialist and advocate for STEM education, particularly for women in technology. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University and has been instrumental in developing AI curricula across UAE universities.

Expertise: Machine Learning, Educational Technology, Women in STEM
Photo

H.E. Dr. Rashid Al-Maktoum

Government Representative

Ministry of Education

H.E. Dr. Al-Maktoum serves as the government liaison, ensuring RoboCup UAE's activities align with national education and innovation strategies. He has been instrumental in integrating robotics into the national curriculum.

Expertise: Education Policy, Government Relations, Strategic Planning
Photo

Eng. Mohammed Al-Qasimi

Industry Representative

Emirates Steel Arkan

Eng. Al-Qasimi brings extensive industry experience in automation and robotics applications. He leads the industrial automation division at Emirates Steel and champions the adoption of robotics in UAE's manufacturing sector.

Expertise: Industrial Automation, Manufacturing, Technology Transfer
Photo

Dr. Mariam Al-Dhaheri

Research Trustee

Masdar Institute

Dr. Al-Dhaheri is a renowned researcher in sustainable robotics and autonomous systems for smart cities. Her work focuses on integrating robotics with renewable energy systems and sustainable urban development.

Expertise: Sustainable Robotics, Smart Cities, Renewable Energy Systems
Photo

Prof. Omar Al-Hashimi

International Relations Trustee

NYU Abu Dhabi

Prof. Al-Hashimi manages RoboCup UAE's international partnerships and collaborations. He serves on several international robotics committees and has been key in establishing UAE's presence in global robotics communities.

Expertise: International Collaboration, Human-Robot Interaction, Service Robotics
Photo

Ms. Aisha Al-Nuaimi

Education Trustee

Dubai Future Foundation

Ms. Al-Nuaimi oversees educational initiatives and outreach programs. She has developed robotics curricula for over 200 schools across the UAE and leads teacher training programs in robotics education.

Expertise: Educational Program Development, Teacher Training, Youth Engagement
Photo

Dr. Khalid Al-Blooshi

Innovation Trustee

Dubai Silicon Oasis

Dr. Al-Blooshi focuses on innovation and entrepreneurship in robotics. He has helped establish 15+ robotics startups and manages the RoboCup UAE innovation fund that supports student entrepreneurs.

Expertise: Innovation Management, Entrepreneurship, Startup Development

Board Committees

Executive Committee

Chair: Dr. Ahmed Al-Mansoori

Handles day-to-day governance and strategic decisions between board meetings.

Academic Affairs Committee

Chair: Prof. Fatima Al-Zahra

Oversees educational programs, research initiatives, and academic partnerships.

Industry Relations Committee

Chair: Eng. Mohammed Al-Qasimi

Manages industry partnerships and technology transfer initiatives.

Finance and Audit Committee

Chair: Dr. Khalid Al-Blooshi

Oversees financial management, budgeting, and audit processes.

Board Governance

Meeting Schedule

The Board of Trustees meets quarterly, with additional meetings called as needed. All meetings follow formal governance procedures with documented minutes and action items.

Term of Service

Board members serve three-year terms, with the possibility of renewal. This ensures continuity while allowing for fresh perspectives and expertise.

Committees

Specialized committees handle specific areas of governance, ensuring detailed oversight and expert guidance in key operational areas.
