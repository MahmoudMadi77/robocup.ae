Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register my team for competitions?

Team registration opens 3 months before each competition. Visit our Events page or contact [email protected] for detailed registration information and requirements.

What are the requirements for participating in RoboCup UAE?

Requirements vary by league. Generally, you need a team of 3-8 members, university/institution affiliation, and robots that meet league specifications. Check individual league pages for detailed requirements.

How can my organization become a partner or sponsor?

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, technology companies, and government organizations. Contact [email protected] to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Are there training programs available for new teams?

Yes, we offer regular workshops, training sessions, and mentorship programs. Check our Events page for upcoming workshops or contact [email protected] for more information.
