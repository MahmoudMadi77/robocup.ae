RoboCupIndustrial

Advancing industrial automation and smart manufacturing through robotics competition

About RoboCupIndustrial

RoboCupIndustrial focuses on the application of robotics and AI in industrial settings. This league addresses challenges in manufacturing, logistics, and industrial automation, promoting the development of intelligent systems for Industry 4.0.

Competition Categories

RoboCup Logistics League (RCLL)

Teams of mobile robots cooperate in a factory automation scenario.

  • 3 robots per team
  • Production planning
  • Multi-robot coordination
  • Real-time decision making

RoboCup@Work

Mobile manipulation robots performing industrial tasks in shop floor scenarios.

  • Mobile manipulation platforms
  • Object recognition and handling
  • Navigation in industrial environments
  • Task planning and execution

Industrial Challenge

Real industrial problems presented by industry partners for innovative solutions.

  • Industry-sponsored challenges
  • Real-world problem solving
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Commercial viability

Simulation League

Virtual industrial environments for testing and developing automation solutions.

  • Digital twin environments
  • Process optimization
  • Virtual commissioning
  • AI-driven automation
