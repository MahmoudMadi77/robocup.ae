RoboCupJunior

Inspiring the next generation of roboticists through educational robotics competitions

About RoboCupJunior

RoboCupJunior is an educational initiative designed to introduce robotics and AI to young students. It provides a platform for students aged 8-19 to learn about robotics, programming, and teamwork through exciting competitions and challenges.

Competition Categories

Soccer

Autonomous robots play football in various divisions based on age and skill level.

  • Soccer Lightweight (2v2)
  • Soccer Open (1v1 or 2v2)
  • Soccer Simulation
  • Age groups: Primary, Secondary

Rescue

Robots navigate through challenging terrains to rescue victims in disaster scenarios.

  • Rescue Line (follow line, avoid obstacles)
  • Rescue Maze (navigate maze, find victims)
  • Rescue Simulation
  • Focus on problem-solving

OnStage

Creative performances combining robotics, art, music, and storytelling.

  • OnStage Preliminary
  • OnStage Advanced
  • Artistic and technical evaluation
  • Creativity and innovation focus

CoSpace

Virtual robotics competition using simulation environments and programming.

  • CoSpace Rescue
  • CoSpace Soccer
  • Programming-focused
  • Accessible to all schools

Age Divisions

Primary Division

Ages 8-14 years

  • Simplified rules and challenges
  • Focus on learning and fun
  • Team-based approach
  • Mentorship encouraged

Secondary Division

Ages 15-19 years

  • Advanced technical challenges
  • Independent problem-solving
  • Research and documentation
  • Preparation for higher education

Educational Benefits

Programming Skills

Learn various programming languages and develop computational thinking.

Understand mechanical design, electronics, and system integration.

Teamwork

Develop collaboration, communication, and leadership skills.

Problem Solving

Enhance critical thinking and creative problem-solving abilities.

Presentation Skills

Learn to document, present, and explain technical solutions.

Global Perspective

Connect with international peers and understand global challenges.
