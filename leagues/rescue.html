RoboCupRescue

Developing robotic solutions for search and rescue operations in disaster scenarios

About RoboCupRescue

RoboCupRescue focuses on developing robotic systems for search and rescue operations in disaster scenarios. This league addresses real-world challenges in emergency response, combining robotics, AI, and multi-agent coordination to save lives.

Competition Categories

Robot League

Physical robots navigate through disaster scenarios to locate and assist victims.

  • Autonomous navigation
  • Victim detection and mapping
  • Debris manipulation
  • Multi-robot coordination

Simulation League

Large-scale disaster simulation with virtual agents coordinating rescue operations.

  • City-wide disaster scenarios
  • Fire brigade coordination
  • Police force management
  • Ambulance team optimization

Infrastructure League

Focus on communication networks and information systems during disasters.

  • Network resilience
  • Information flow optimization
  • Resource allocation
  • Decision support systems

Space Robotics

Robotic systems for extreme environments and space-based rescue operations.

  • Extreme environment navigation
  • Remote operation capabilities
  • Autonomous decision making
  • Resource management

Technical Challenges

SLAM & Navigation

Simultaneous localization and mapping in unknown, dynamic environments

Victim Detection

Identifying and locating victims using various sensors and AI techniques

Multi-Agent Systems

Coordinating multiple robots and agents in complex scenarios

Manipulation

Moving debris and obstacles to access victims and clear paths

Real-World Applications

Fire Response

Robots assisting firefighters in dangerous environments, providing reconnaissance and support.

Building Collapse

Search and rescue operations in collapsed structures, locating survivors efficiently.

Flood Response

Aquatic robots for water-based rescue operations and flood monitoring.

Natural Disasters

Response to earthquakes, landslides, and other natural catastrophes.
