Executive Committee

Strategic Leadership & Governance

The Executive Committee provides strategic direction and overall governance for RoboCup UAE. They oversee all major decisions, partnerships, and long-term planning.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Strategic planning and vision setting
  • Partnership development and management
  • Budget oversight and financial planning
  • Policy development and governance
  • International relations with RoboCup Federation

Technical Committee

Competition Rules & Standards

The Technical Committee ensures fair play and maintains high technical standards across all RoboCup UAE competitions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Competition rule development and updates
  • Technical standard setting and enforcement
  • Robot inspection and certification
  • Innovation evaluation and recognition
  • Technical dispute resolution

Education Committee

Learning & Development

The Education Committee focuses on promoting robotics education and developing training programs.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Educational program development
  • Workshop and training coordination
  • Curriculum development support
  • Teacher and mentor training
  • Educational resource creation

Junior Committee

Youth Development & Engagement

The Junior Committee is dedicated to nurturing young talent in robotics and overseeing RoboCup Junior competitions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • RoboCup Junior competition management
  • Youth program development
  • School outreach initiatives
  • Mentor training for junior teams
  • Age-appropriate challenge design

Industry Relations Committee

Corporate Partnerships & Innovation

The Industry Relations Committee builds bridges between academia and industry, facilitating partnerships and sponsorships.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Corporate partnership development
  • Sponsorship coordination
  • Industry challenge design
  • Technology transfer facilitation
  • Career development programs

Media & Communications Committee

Outreach & Public Relations

The Media & Communications Committee manages all external communications and promotes RoboCup UAE activities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Media relations and press coverage
  • Social media management
  • Website content development
  • Marketing campaign coordination
  • Public awareness initiatives

Events Committee

Competition Organization & Logistics

The Events Committee handles all aspects of competition organization and logistics for smooth event execution.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Event planning and coordination
  • Venue management and logistics
  • Registration and participant services
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Safety and security management

International Affairs Committee

Global Collaboration & Exchange

The International Affairs Committee manages relationships with the global RoboCup community and facilitates international collaboration.

Key Responsibilities:

  • International competition coordination
  • Global partnership development
  • Exchange program management
  • International standards alignment
  • Cultural exchange facilitation
