Regional Partners

Building bridges across the Middle East and beyond

RoboCup UAE serves as a regional hub for robotics education and competition, fostering partnerships across the Middle East, North Africa, and South Asia. Our collaborative approach strengthens the global robotics community while promoting knowledge sharing and cultural exchange.

Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) Partners

KSA

Saudi Arabia Robotics Society

Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Leading robotics organization in Saudi Arabia, organizing national competitions and promoting STEM education across the Kingdom.

Collaboration Areas:
  • Joint competitions and events
  • Student exchange programs
  • Research collaborations
  • Technical knowledge sharing
QAT

Qatar Robotics Federation

Doha, Qatar

National body for robotics in Qatar, focusing on educational robotics and supporting Qatar's National Vision 2030.

Collaboration Areas:
  • Gulf Robotics Championship
  • Educator training programs
  • Technology transfer initiatives
KWT

Kuwait Institute for Scientific Research

Kuwait City, Kuwait

Premier research institution in Kuwait with strong robotics and automation programs.

Collaboration Areas:
  • Industrial robotics research
  • Joint publications
  • Conference participation
BHR

Bahrain Robotics Society

Manama, Bahrain

Promoting robotics education and innovation in Bahrain through competitions and educational programs.

OMN

Oman Robotics Club

Muscat, Oman

National robotics organization supporting STEM education and robotics competitions in Oman.

Middle East & North Africa Partners

EGY

Egyptian Robotics Federation

Cairo, Egypt

Leading robotics organization in Egypt with strong university partnerships and active competition programs.

Key Initiatives:
  • MENA Robotics Championship
  • Arabic robotics curriculum development
  • Regional judge training programs
JOR

Jordan Robotics Society

Amman, Jordan

National robotics organization promoting STEM education and hosting regional competitions.

LBN

Lebanese Robotics Association

Beirut, Lebanon

Fostering robotics innovation and education in Lebanon through university partnerships.

MAR

Morocco Robotics Initiative

Rabat, Morocco

Promoting robotics education and research across Morocco with focus on French-Arabic bilingual programs.

RoboCup Federation

Global

The international governing body for RoboCup competitions worldwide, providing standards, guidelines, and global coordination.

Partnership Benefits:
  • Access to international competitions
  • Technical standards and guidelines
  • Global networking opportunities
  • Best practices sharing
SGP

RoboCup Singapore

Singapore

Leading Asian RoboCup organization with expertise in educational robotics and competition management.

AUS

RoboCup Australia

Australia

Established RoboCup organization with strong research focus and innovative competition formats.

GER

Germany

One of the largest RoboCup competitions in Europe, providing valuable experience and networking opportunities.
