About RoboCup UAE

Pioneering the future of robotics and AI in the United Arab Emirates

Our Mission

RoboCup UAE is dedicated to advancing robotics and artificial intelligence research and education throughout the United Arab Emirates. We serve as the national organizing body for RoboCup competitions, fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence in autonomous robotics.

What We Do

  • Organize national and regional RoboCup competitions
  • Support teams participating in international RoboCup events
  • Promote STEM education through robotics programs
  • Facilitate research collaboration between universities and industry
  • Develop the next generation of robotics engineers and researchers

Our Vision

To establish the UAE as a leading hub for robotics and AI innovation in the Middle East, contributing to the nation's vision of becoming a global leader in technology and innovation.
