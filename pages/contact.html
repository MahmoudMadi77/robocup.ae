Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register for RoboCup UAE?

Registration opens annually in March. Visit our competitions page for detailed information and registration forms.

What are the age requirements?

RoboCup Junior is for ages 8-19, while the main competition is open to university students and professionals of all ages.

Do you provide training workshops?

Yes! We offer regular workshops throughout the year covering robotics fundamentals, AI programming, and competition strategies.

Can international teams participate?

Absolutely! We welcome teams from around the world to participate in RoboCup UAE competitions.

What equipment do I need?

Equipment requirements vary by league. Check our competition guidelines for specific technical requirements for each category.

How can my company sponsor RoboCup UAE?

We offer various sponsorship packages. Contact us directly to discuss partnership opportunities that align with your goals.
