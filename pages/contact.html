Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register my team for RoboCup competitions?

Team registration typically opens 3-4 months before each competition. Visit our Events page for current registration information and deadlines. You can also contact our Competitions department at competitions@robocup.ae for specific requirements.

What support is available for new teams?

We offer comprehensive support including technical workshops, mentorship programs, and educational resources. Contact our Education & Training department for information about upcoming workshops and training opportunities.

How can my organization become a sponsor or partner?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision for advancing robotics and AI. Contact our Partnerships department at partnerships@robocup.ae to discuss sponsorship opportunities and collaboration possibilities.

Are there opportunities for international collaboration?

Yes! We actively collaborate with international RoboCup organizations and research institutions. Contact our Research & Development department to explore collaboration opportunities in research, education, and competition organization.
