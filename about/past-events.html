RoboCup UAE 2024
April 12-15, 2024

RoboCup UAE 2024

Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai

The largest RoboCup UAE competition to date, featuring 45 teams from 12 universities across all five leagues. Introduced new AI challenges and industry partnerships.

45 Teams 12 Universities 5 Leagues

Champions:

  • Soccer: AUS Robotics (American University of Sharjah)
  • Rescue: UAEU Rescue Team (UAE University)
  • @Home: NYU Abu Dhabi Assistants
Junior Championship 2024
June 8-10, 2024

RoboCup Junior UAE Championship 2024

Khalifa University, Abu Dhabi

National championship for junior teams with record participation from 25 schools. Three teams qualified for RoboCup Junior World Championship in Netherlands.

25 Schools 60 Teams 180 Students

Notable Achievements

International Success

UAE teams have won 15+ awards at international RoboCup competitions, including 3 world championship titles in junior categories.

Research Impact

Over 200 research papers published by participating teams, contributing to global robotics and AI knowledge base.

Industry Innovation

12 startups launched by RoboCup UAE alumni, creating jobs and driving innovation in the UAE's tech sector.

Educational Impact

Reached over 5,000 students through competitions and workshops, inspiring the next generation of roboticists.
