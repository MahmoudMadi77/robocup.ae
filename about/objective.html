Our Objective

Driving innovation and excellence in robotics and AI across the UAE

Primary Mission

RoboCup UAE aims to advance the state of the art of intelligent robots and foster innovation in robotics and artificial intelligence throughout the United Arab Emirates, supporting the nation's vision of becoming a global leader in AI and smart technologies by 2071.

Our Vision for 2030

Leading the Future

By 2030, we envision RoboCup UAE as the premier platform for robotics innovation in the Middle East, with:

  • 100+ participating teams annually
  • Partnerships with all major UAE universities
  • International recognition as a center of excellence
  • Direct contribution to UAE's AI strategy goals
  • Successful technology transfer to industry

Strategic Goals

Education: Reach 10,000+ students through robotics programs
Research: Publish 100+ peer-reviewed papers annually
Industry: Launch 20+ robotics startups from our ecosystem
Global: Host international RoboCup events in the UAE

Alignment with National Vision

UAE Vision 2071

Supporting the UAE's goal to become the world's best country by 2071 through excellence in education, economy, and government services powered by AI and robotics.

AI Strategy 2031

Contributing to the UAE's National Strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031, which aims to position the UAE as a global leader in AI across various sectors.

Fourth Industrial Revolution

Preparing the UAE workforce for the Fourth Industrial Revolution through advanced robotics education and practical applications in smart cities and industries.

Expected Impact

Economic Impact

Contributing to UAE's knowledge economy through innovation, job creation, and technology commercialization in robotics and AI sectors.

Social Impact

Enhancing quality of life through robotics applications in healthcare, education, transportation, and smart city services.

Educational Impact

Transforming STEM education and creating pathways for students to pursue careers in emerging technologies and robotics.
