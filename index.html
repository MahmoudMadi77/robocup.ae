About RoboCup UAE

RoboCup UAE is the national chapter of the international RoboCup initiative, dedicated to advancing the state of the art of intelligent robots in the United Arab Emirates. Established to promote robotics education, research, and innovation across the UAE, we organize competitions and events that bring together students, researchers, and robotics enthusiasts.

Our mission aligns with the UAE's Vision 2071 and the National Strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031, fostering a culture of innovation and technological advancement in robotics and AI.

RoboCup UAE Domain Leagues

RoboCupSoccer

Autonomous robots playing soccer in various categories including Humanoid, Standard Platform, Middle Size, Small Size, and Simulation leagues.

  • Humanoid League
  • Standard Platform League
  • Middle Size League
  • Small Size League
  • Simulation League

RoboCupRescue

Robots designed for search and rescue operations in disaster scenarios, testing both physical robots and simulation systems.

  • Robot League
  • Simulation League

RoboCup@Home

Service robots operating in domestic environments, focusing on human-robot interaction and practical applications.

  • Open Platform League
  • Domestic Standard Platform
  • Social Standard Platform

RoboCupIndustrial

Industrial automation and logistics challenges for robots in manufacturing and warehouse environments.

  • RoboCup@Work
  • Logistics League

RoboCupJunior

Educational robotics competitions for students aged 14-19, fostering STEM education and innovation.
