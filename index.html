About RoboCup UAE

Fostering innovation and excellence in robotics across the Emirates

Our Mission

RoboCup UAE is dedicated to advancing robotics and artificial intelligence research, education, and innovation throughout the United Arab Emirates. We organize world-class competitions that challenge teams to push the boundaries of what's possible in robotics.

Our Vision

To establish the UAE as a leading hub for robotics innovation in the Middle East, fostering collaboration between academia, industry, and government to create solutions that benefit society.
